RAG Knowledge Agent

Knowledge Base

Turn your local documents, PDFs, Notion pages, and shared drives into an intelligent, Q&A chat assistant that answers with absolute accuracy and exact citations.

Required Inputs

  • Ingestion Sources: PDF, DOCX, Notion, or Google Drive folder sync.
  • Query text: Question from user.
  • Access tokens: Google Drive or Confluence permissions.

Expected Outputs

  • Answer: Synthesis based strictly on reference context.
  • Citations: Filename, page number, and source URL reference.
  • Confidence Score: Retrieval similarity value.

How to Use

  1. Connect your document sources in your dashboard panel.
  2. Wait for the vector indexing engine to chunk and embed files.
  3. Ask questions directly in the web chat widget, Slack, or Telegram.

Data Pipeline & Process

1 Document Upload
2 Embedding & Vector Store
3 Similarity Search
4 LLM Citation Synthesis

Marketing Content Agent

Marketing

Generate ready-to-publish, multi-channel social media posts, email newsletters, and blog drafts starting from a single content campaign brief.

Required Inputs

  • Campaign brief: Subject topic, core messaging points.
  • Target Channels: LinkedIn, Instagram, X (Twitter), Blog, or Email.
  • Brand Tone: Past top-performing posts style.

Expected Outputs

  • Draft variants: Multi-channel formatted text variants.
  • Visual assets: DALL·E 3 generated cover image concept prompts.
  • Republish Queue: Direct sync to Buffer/Meta calendar slots.

How to Use

  1. Input your campaign brief details and select target outputs.
  2. Click "Generate" and review the draft variations in your editor.
  3. Edit or click "Publish" to schedule the posts onto your channels.

Data Pipeline & Process

1 Brief Submission
2 Channel Adaptation
3 Brand Verification
4 Schedule / Publish

Stock Market Agent

Finance

Sync with broker holding portfolios to track fundamentals, news indicators, and technical charts, surfacing real-time unusual movement notifications.

Required Inputs

  • Broker linkage: Read-only broker API or CSV upload.
  • Watchlist symbols: Specific stock tickers.
  • Alert thresholds: Price/Volume change triggers.

Expected Outputs

  • Morning Briefing: Summary watchlist reports before market open.
  • Real-time alerts: Slack, Webhook, or Telegram text notifications.
  • Source documentation: Direct links to financial reports or filings.

How to Use

  1. Link your brokerage account securely using read-only API access.
  2. Define triggers for stock watchlists.
  3. Configure notifications to hit your workspace channel (Slack/Telegram).

Data Pipeline & Process

1 Portfolio Synchronization
2 TA & News Crawling
3 Signal Analysis
4 Alert Dispatch

HR Recruitment Agent

HR

Automate candidate screening by scanning CVs against active job descriptions, scoring fit, and initiating interview calendar schedulers.

Required Inputs

  • Job Description: Active role must-haves and criteria.
  • CV files: PDF resume uploads from applicants.
  • Hiring Slots: Recruiter calendar availability.

Expected Outputs

  • Scorecards: Structured fit score and evaluation trails.
  • Shortlists: Top candidates flagged for interview.
  • Scheduled slots: Live calendar invites via Cal.com.

How to Use

  1. Upload job requirements and criteria guidelines.
  2. Connect application pipelines (email inbox or ATS integrations).
  3. Review scores on the candidate board and confirm calendar bookings.

Data Pipeline & Process

1 CV Parsing
2 Must-Haves Scoring
3 Fit Evaluation
4 Calendar Scheduling

Customer Review Responder

Reputation Management

Monitor customer reviews across Google, Yelp, and Amazon, generate custom responses based on review sentiments, and reply instantly with one-click approval.

Required Inputs

  • Listing Sync: Google Business, Yelp, or Amazon seller API.
  • Guidelines: Brand voice guidelines, response policies.
  • Customer reviews: Newly received review stars and text.

Expected Outputs

  • Draft responses: Tone-aligned answers ready for publish.
  • Alert triggers: Notifications for negative review reviewals.
  • Sentiment metrics: Performance feedback statistics.

How to Use

  1. Link Google, Yelp, or Amazon listings to active reviewer agents.
  2. Review incoming draft responses in your central approval feed.
  3. Click "Approve" to post response to customer listings via APIs.

Data Pipeline & Process

1 Review Monitoring
2 Sentiment Analysis
3 Drafting Response
4 Approved Publish

Appointment Booking Agent

Service Business

Conversational booking agent that handles customer booking requests, reminders, and reschedules synced across Twilio Voice, WhatsApp, and web calendar APIs.

Required Inputs

  • Staff slots: Live availability synced to team calendars.
  • Customer detail: Name, contact details, requested service.
  • Payment details: Razorpay / Stripe billing credentials.

Expected Outputs

  • Calendar entries: Synced event reservations in team schedules.
  • Reminders: Automated WhatsApp reminder notifications.
  • Bills: Payment confirmations and structured receipts.

How to Use

  1. Connect team calendar feeds and define operational slots.
  2. Embed booking widgets on web pages or deploy to WhatsApp numbers.
  3. Monitor active schedules and review reminders through calendars.

Data Pipeline & Process

1 Booking Query
2 Availability Verification
3 Payment Processing
4 Calendar Reservation
